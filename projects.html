Dalmiya House Margao - Injection Cement Grouting

Injection grouting is the method of filling cracks, open joints, voids, or honeycombs in concrete or masonry structural members. This is done under pressure with a grout material that cures in place to strengthen the structure and prevent water ingress.

Grout is a flowable plastic material and should have negligible shrinkage so it can fully fill gaps and voids while remaining stable without cracking, delamination, or crumbling. This project reflects the dependable execution standards of Sabila Waterproofing Enterprises.

The Westin Goa, Anjuna

At The Westin Goa, Anjuna, comprehensive injection grouting and waterproofing solutions were implemented to reinforce the structure against heavy rainfall, high humidity, and saline air.

Specialized polyurethane and epoxy grouts were injected under pressure to fill structural cracks, joints, and cavities. This provided effective sealing and prevented water ingress in basements, swimming pool structures, and key service areas.

Premium protective coatings were also applied to safeguard both exterior and interior surfaces. UV-resistant elastomeric coatings protected exterior walls, antimicrobial coatings supported hygienic interior spaces, and epoxy coatings added chemical and abrasion resistance in kitchens and utility areas.

These advanced waterproofing and coating systems help The Westin Goa maintain long-term structural protection while preserving its luxury finish and aesthetic excellence.
